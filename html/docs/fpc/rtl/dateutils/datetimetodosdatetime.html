Description

DateTimeToDosDatetime takes Value, a TDateTime formatted timestamp, and recodes it to a MS-DOS encoded date/time value. This is a longint with the date/time encoded in the bits as:

0-4
Seconds divided by 2
5-10
Minutes
11-15
Hours
16-20
Day
21-24
Month
25-31
Years since 1980
