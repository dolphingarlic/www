DecodeDate

Decode a TDateTime to a year,month,day triplet

Declaration

procedure DecodeDate(

  Date: TDateTime;

  out Month: Word;

Description

DecodeDate decodes the Year, Month and Day stored in Date, and returns them in the Year, Month and Day variables.

Errors

None.
